I do not think I have seen this earlier, but I do not visit the homepage regularly. But congratulations to the
[CrystalEye](http://wwmm.ch.cam.ac.uk/crystaleye/) team for releasing the data explicitly as
[PDDL](http://www.opendatacommons.org/licenses/pddl/1-0/)! I cannot stress enough how useful it is if you add
a statement like this to your *public domain* data. Well done!
