I am reorganizing my blogs, and will now take advantage of the [labels concept](http://www.google.com/support/blogger/bin/topic.py?hl=en&topic=12459)
of [Blogger](http://www.blogger.com/). The new URLs are:

* [Chemical blogspace](http://chemicalblogspace.blogspot.com/) becomes [http://chem-bla-ics.blogspot.com/search/label/chemicalblogspace](http://chem-bla-ics.blogspot.com/search/label/chemicalblogspace)
* [A Chemical RCP Adventure](http://chemicalrcp.blogspot.com/) becomes [http://chem-bla-ics.blogspot.com/search/label/planeteclipse](http://chem-bla-ics.blogspot.com/search/label/planeteclipse)
* [Kemistry Desktop Environment](http://kemistry-desktop.blogspot.com/) becomes [http://chem-bla-ics.blogspot.com/search/label/kemistry](http://chem-bla-ics.blogspot.com/search/label/kemistry)

And likewise for one or two [other](http://metabolomicsinnl.blogspot.com/) [blogs](http://cmlexplained.blogspot.com/).
Not all labels are used yet, and I might repost some old posts, or perhaps write up some new content.
